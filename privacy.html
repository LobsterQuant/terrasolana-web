Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Terrasolana ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Email address — if you subscribe to our newsletter or submit a contact form.
  • Name and enquiry details — if you request a buyer's agent introduction.
  • Usage data — page views and interaction data via Plausible Analytics (privacy-friendly, no cookies, no personal identifiers).

How we use it

  • To send you the weekly newsletter (email subscribers).
  • To connect you with a buyer's agent partner (enquiry form submissions).
  • To understand which content and features are most useful.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't use cookies for advertising or tracking.
  • We don't store financial or payment information.

Third parties

We use Formspree to process form submissions and Plausible for analytics. Both are GDPR-compliant. Newsletter delivery is handled via Beehiiv.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email. To request deletion of your data, email us at contact@terrasolana.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact@terrasolana.com
