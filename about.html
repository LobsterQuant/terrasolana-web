About Terrasolana

We built the Spain property search we couldn't find anywhere else.

Idealista has four million listings. Fotocasa has three million more. Kyero has another 163,000. None of them will tell you the rental yield. None of them will tell you whether the asking price is 15% above the neighbourhood median. None of them will flag that this property has been sitting for 60 days with two price drops — and the seller is motivated.

They're built to maximise listings and agent revenue. Not to help buyers make good decisions.

Terrasolana is different. We're a filter layer, not a marketplace. We scan the major Spanish portals, run every property through our scoring model, and surface only the listings that pass. Every property on Terrasolana has real investment metrics attached — gross yield, net yield, estimated rent, price vs. neighbourhood median, days on market, and an opportunity score.

Who we're for

Terrasolana is for buyers who want to make a good decision, not just a quick one. For the Dutch couple buying their first buy-to-let. For the British investor looking for yield outside London. For the German buyer who wants to see the numbers before the viewings. For anyone serious about Spain — not just browsing.

We cover Valencia, Madrid, Málaga, Alicante, and Almería — five very different markets with very different yield profiles. We'll expand as our data improves.

How our data works

Our yield calculations, opportunity scores, and market comparisons are explained in full on our Methodology page →. We believe in transparency about what the numbers mean and where they come from.

How we make money

When a buyer uses our "connect with agent" feature and goes on to purchase a property, we receive a referral fee from the buyer's agent — typically 15–20% of their commission. This is disclosed transparently. Our agents don't pay to list; we refer buyers to the agents we trust.

We also publish a free weekly newsletter. In the future we may offer premium data reports for institutional buyers and developers.
